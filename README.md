## Brand at a Glance

- **Primary colors:** Black `#0D0D0D`, White `#FFFFFF`, Levici Blue `#479ECD`, Silver `#B0B0B0`
- **Fonts:** AZONIX (headings), Quicksand (body)
- **Style:** Minimal, symmetrical, clean. Blue used sparingly.
- **Photography:** Black & white until new studio photos available.
